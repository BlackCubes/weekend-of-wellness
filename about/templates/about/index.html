Community is everything.

They aren't kidding when they say "It takes a Village."

Here in Kingsburg... it takes a Swedish Village.

This event has been made possible for the community by the community; the Weekend of Wellness is paving the way for a future where all individuals feel encouraged, supported and inspired within their health journeys and paths towards wellness.

What is Wellness?

Wellness takes many forms; mental health, physical health, and nutritional wellbeing. Wellness is not limited to these three areas. In fact, social connection and community engagement are two of the most important aspects of wellness. This is why community is so important to us at the Kingsburg Weekend of Wellness event.

As you take part in our Weekend of Wellness, our goal is to discuss, educate, and inspire you to take control of your wellness journey. As a result of our classes, farmers market, pickleball tournament, and more, we hope that you learn ways to connect with community members, organizations, and healthcare professionals who desire to assist you as you seek a future of a healthier lifestyle.

Your path towards wellness can start at any age, and we encourage you to begin this journey with us by bringing your families along, and becoming informed together. A bright, healthy future lies ahead, and we are so excited to be a part of your growth!
