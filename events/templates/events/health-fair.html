Join Us!

The Health Fair highlights local professionals, businesses, and organizations within the community that are here to support you and your wellness journey. Located at the north-east end of the park, the Health Fair provides an opportunity for you to engage in conversations with presenters and booths that are interesting to you.

Whether you are looking to try out a new gym nearby, curious about health providers in our area, are looking to receive a free blood-pressure or glucose reading, or would like to get involved within health and wellness in our community, come on by and see what the Health Fair has to offer for you!
