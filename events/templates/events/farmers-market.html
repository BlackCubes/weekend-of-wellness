Join Us!

The Farmers Market highlights local farmers, food vendors and businesses within the community that are here to support nutritional wellness. It will be located at the south-east end of the park, adjacent to our Health Fair.

Come try some of the best eats of the Valley and purchase locally grown produce and commodities to enjoy at home.

Want to get involved?

We are inviting local farmers, food vendors, and food trucks to be a part of the Farmers Market.

We hope to encourage our community to shop for local produce, purchase healthy food options and enjoy a nutritious meal at our event. If your business is rooted in a similar vision, we would love for you to be a part of our Farmers Market.
